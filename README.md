#### Hi there, I'm Katrin 👋

I’m a biologist currently in the final stages of my PhD at CCMAR (University of the Algarve in Portugal), based in the Frankfurt region, Germany. 🇩🇪
My current projects orbit around a central question: how do holobionts work at the genetic level? To explore this, I dive into long-read sequencing (Oxford Nanopore Technologies), short-read sequencing (Illumina), and phylogenetics/phylogenomics.
Alongside my background in molecular biology (over 15 years), I enjoy self-teaching and exploring the "other side" of biology – bioinformatics. It is challenging, but equally fascinating and rewarding. I am constantly expanding my skill set, mainly in Python, Bash, and R.
My repositories will grow, improve, and change as I learn. If something looks unfinished today, it will be better tomorrow. Feedback and collaborations are always welcome!
